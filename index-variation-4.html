What We Do

Comprehensive Fire Alarm Services

From first blueprint to final inspection, we deliver code-compliant fire protection systems built to last.

Preconstruction Planning & Design

We work with architects and engineers from day one, producing code-compliant fire alarm designs under IBC, IFC, and NFPA standards before a single wire is run.

Fire Alarm Installation

Our UL-listed installations (UL-UUJS, UL-UUFX) and NICET-certified technicians ensure your system meets the highest national safety standards.

Inspection, Testing & Compliance

We handle all post-installation inspection, testing, city submittals, and compliance documentation so you never have to chase paperwork.
