About J Webb Inc

Lawrence's Trusted Life Safety Experts

J Webb Inc — operating as Life Safety Systems — has been protecting buildings and the people inside them for over 30 years. Based in Lawrence, Kansas, we specialize exclusively in fire alarm systems: from preconstruction design through installation and long-term service.

We aren't a general contractor who does fire alarms on the side. This is all we do — and that singular focus means our work is held to a higher standard. Every system we deliver is code-compliant, UL-listed, and built to protect lives for years to come.

Code-First Design Every plan is built to IBC, IFC, and NFPA standards from day one — no retrofits, no surprises at inspection.
Local Expertise, Long-Term Relationships We've worked alongside Lawrence architects, GCs, and building owners for decades — we know the projects, the people, and the expectations.
Full-Cycle Service Design through installation through annual inspection — one company handles it all, with no handoffs and no gaps in accountability.
